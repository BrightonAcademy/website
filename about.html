About Brighton Academy

Brighton Academy is a premium tuition center in Anna Nagar, Chennai, dedicated to delivering personalized, high-quality academic coaching for students pursuing IB, IGCSE, and CBSE curriculums. Trusted by parents from CPS Global, Olive International, and Achiever Global, we’re known for our results-driven approach and expert faculty.

Our Mission

To empower students with the academic tools, mentorship, and confidence they need to succeed in competitive school environments and beyond. We focus on building strong conceptual understanding, consistent discipline, and personalized learning journeys.
